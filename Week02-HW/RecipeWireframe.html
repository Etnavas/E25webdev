Dominican Flan Recipe

How to make a classic Dominican flan

ingredients:

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 table spoon of water
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 and a half cup of condensed
  • 1 and a half cup of evaporated milk

instructions

  1. Pre-heat the over at 320 ÂºF
  2. Mix together the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and evaporated milk into a bowl.
  3. Stir all ingredients until they are all convined evenly the set it aside
  4. On sauce pan add the sugar and the water and on low heat mix both ingredients until a dark thick caramel forms then add the mixture into a mold
  5. Take the mixture you set aside earlier and carefully pour it on to of the caramel mixture in the mold
  6. Bake in the oven for an hour or until you insert a toothpick and it comes out clean
  7. After taking it out of the oven let it cool to room temperature, afterwards let it chill in a fridge or a freezer
  8. Take it out of the oven and loosen up the sides of the flan, place a plate on top, and flip the dessert out of the mold
